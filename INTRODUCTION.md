# RimMind - Storyteller

AI 叙事导演，根据殖民地历史编织连贯的故事线。

## 核心能力

**历史感知事件选择** - AI 了解殖民地过去发生了什么，据此选择有叙事连贯性的下一个事件，而非纯随机。

**事件链系统** - AI 可以创建多步连锁事件，编织有起承转合的故事弧线。

**张力系统** - 追踪殖民地紧张程度，事件触发时增加，自然衰减，实现张弛有度的叙事节奏。

**事件记忆系统** - 记录已发生事件的历史，包括时间、类型、结果，为 AI 决策提供时间线上下文。

**智能候选生成** - 向 AI 展示当前可能触发的事件列表，让 AI 从中选择最符合叙事逻辑的一个。

**Fallback 机制** - AI 不可用时自动切换到经典叙事者（Cassandra / Randy / Phoebe），游戏不会中断。

**叙事者祭坛** - 游戏内建筑，玩家可交互主动触发 AI 叙事，推动剧情发展。

**自定义叙事风格** - 通过 Prompt 自定义 AI 的叙事偏好，从冷酷到温和任你选择。

## 叙事特色

- 悲剧之后可能迎来希望
- 长期平静后酝酿风暴
- 根据殖民地特点定制事件（农业殖民地遭遇作物病害）
- 避免重复同类事件造成疲劳
- 连锁事件创造有记忆点的故事线

# RimMind - Storyteller (English)

An AI narrative director weaving coherent storylines based on colony history.

## Key Features

**History-Aware Event Selection** - AI understands what has happened in the colony and chooses the next event with narrative coherence rather than pure randomness.

**Event Chain System** - AI can create multi-step chained events, weaving story arcs with beginnings, developments, and climaxes.

**Tension System** - Tracks colony tension level, increasing with events and naturally decaying, creating dramatic pacing with ebbs and flows.

**Event Memory System** - Records history of occurred events, including time, type, and outcome, providing timeline context for AI decisions.

**Smart Candidate Generation** - Shows AI a list of currently triggerable events, letting AI choose the one that best fits narrative logic.

**Fallback Mechanism** - Automatically switches to classic storyteller (Cassandra / Randy / Phoebe) when AI is unavailable. Game never interrupts.

**Storyteller Altar** - In-game building where players can interact to trigger AI narrative events, actively pushing the story forward.

**Custom Narrative Style** - Define AI's narrative preferences through prompts, from ruthless to gentle.

## Narrative Features

- Hope may follow tragedy
- Storms brew after long calm
- Events tailored to colony characteristics (agricultural colony faces crop blight)
- Avoids repetition fatigue from similar events
- Chained events create memorable storylines
